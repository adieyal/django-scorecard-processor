2012 IHP+Results Monitoring Process Online Survey Tool

Welcome to the 3rd annual monitoring of the International Health Partnership (IHP+) Global Compact commitments, by IHP+Results. You can use this online survey tool to submit data to IHP+Results on your organisations performance. Alternatively you can use an Excel tool. But there are certain advantages to completing the survey online:

  1. Your organisation’s data will be automatically saved within our database so you do not have to store and find multiple Excel files (ie we manage your version control!).
  2. If you are managing or overseeing returns from many countries, you can access them all here, and switch between them without having to open different files.
  3. Participants from the 2nd round of IHP+ monitoring (in 2010) can see their previously submitted data as reminder of how you tackled the exercise previously.
  4. You will be prompted if you enter an unexpected piece of data (e.g. entering text into a numeric field). This should promote consistency across all survey responses.
  5. You can access key terms and definitions in the same space rather than having to look them up in a separate document.

To use the survey tool you need to be registered. Registration is managed by the IHP+Results team in conjunction with IHP+ focal points in all participating organisations. Please contact your IHP+ focal point if you need to be registered to use this system. You may already have been registered, in which you case you will have received an email from ‘IHP+Results Survey 2012’; please click on the link in that email and follow the on screen instructions to access the online survey.
